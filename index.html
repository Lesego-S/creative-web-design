About US

Ming Wu Wei is an acclaimed Seoulite Interior Designer with over a decade of experience.
Her design philosophy is rooted in minimalism, blending functionality with aesthetic elegance.
Ming's approach focuses on creating spaces that are not only visually captivating but also enhance the lifestyle of their occupants.
With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to quality, Ming Wu Wei has established herself as a sought-after
professional in the field, known for her ability to transform spaces into harmonious environments that inspire and delight.
